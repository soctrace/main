# Mijas Political Context Pack

## Source

The pack derives from the internal expert document
`municipality_packs/mijas/source_documents/Contexto histórico elecciones municipales en Mijas.docx`.
The source is not treated as a verified factual dataset.

## Retrieval Structure

- `historical_context.md` summarizes territorial settlement patterns.
- `political_context_structured.md` separates concise electoral facts from hypotheses.
- `contextual_hypotheses.yaml` stores machine-readable priors with confidence and caution.
- `forecast_counterweights_methodology.md` defines the model-use boundary.

## Scientific Boundary

Context never replaces observed data and never becomes deterministic. Opinions,
claims about motives and descriptions of individuals in the source document are
excluded from factual retrieval. Future political supply hypotheses remain
inactive until human-confirmed.

The analyst must state which part of an explanation comes from observed
electoral results, synthetic socioeconomic or housing features, contextual
hypotheses and unresolved uncertainty.
