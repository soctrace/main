# Forecast Counterweights Methodology

Context never replaces data. The counterweights mart calculates observed
cross-election gaps, party-floor stability, declining-party pools and localist
signals from approved electoral marts. Territorial context adds bounded
sensitivity and uncertainty.

Candidate reset is a cautious hypothesis-based potential. Conservative localist
split risk is stored but inactive until human-confirmed electoral supply exists.
Cs and Por Mi Pueblo pools are not mechanically transferred to any party.

The forecast exposes three layers:

1. Structural forecast from approved electoral and synthetic data.
2. Bounded contextual counterweight adjustment.
3. Final interpreted estimate with confidence reduced by contextual uncertainty.
