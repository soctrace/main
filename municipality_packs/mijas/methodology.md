# Methodology

Forecast outputs are estimated structural baselines generated from approved
internal marts. They are not polls and must be explained with uncertainty,
confidence and model drivers. Oraculum calibration is reserved for validated
future inputs.

When contextual counterweights are present, explanations must distinguish:

- observed electoral data;
- synthetic socioeconomic, housing or demographic features;
- bounded contextual hypotheses;
- unresolved uncertainty.

Never present a candidate-quality opinion, political motive or future party
supply hypothesis as a fact.
